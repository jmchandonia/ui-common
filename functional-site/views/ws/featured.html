KBase Narrative Apps

New KBase Apps Are Coming Soon!

KBase is currently undergoing a major update. We are developing a set of Narrative "apps" that will make it easy to upload and analyze your data with KBase's powerful analysis tools. These apps will be ready-to-use short Narratives that perform a series of useful steps (for example, assemble and annotate a genome). They can be used individually or connected together to form more complex Narratives.
